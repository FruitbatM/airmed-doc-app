AIRMED Clinic Mission and Values

The mission of AIRMED CLINIC is to improve the health of the community and the world by setting the standard of excellence in medical education, research and clinical care. Our mission is to enable more personal, more affordable and more efficient ways of giving care that unleashes the expertise and creativity of our providers and supports the gathering of patients and providers at the times and places best suited to their needs.

In the delivery of our care, through our research and within our communities, AIRMED CLINIC is committed to the well-being of our patients locally and globally. We bridge innovation science with state-of-the-art clinical medicine. We offer high-quality, coordinated care for patients and families via phone, video and email.

We provide care locally, nationally and globally by partnering with underserved communities to build, improve and sustain health care delivery and healthier communities. Our approach to patient care encompasses our commitment to safety, effectiveness, patient care, timeliness, efficiency and equity - an approach that is always evolving based on the latest research.

World-Class Patient Care

AIRMED CLINIC is consistently ranked among the top hospitals in Ireland. Our patients have access to a vast network of physicians, nearly all of whom are Harvard Medical School faculty and many of whom are leaders within their fields. Our patients rate their experiences with us very highly, with nearly all indicating they would be very likely to recommend us to others. We also consistently achieve high ratings from professional organizations.

AIRMED CLINIC was founded to serve our neighbors and those in need, a commitment that is just as strong today as it was 10 years ago. Our community today extends far beyond Dublin's border. We partner with communities both close to home and across oceans to build, improve and sustain health care delivery and increase the health and well-being of those who live and work in these communities.
